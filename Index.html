The Story Behind the Stories

Some summers, ice water gets thrown on sleeping children. Some courtrooms witness GoPro-wearing husbands declaring war on authority. Some therapy sessions end with iPad love poems that nobody asked for.

This is what happens when bipolar disorder meets a marriage, when mania creates organizational systems for containers, when reality becomes so surreal you have to document it or risk losing your mind entirely.

"The summer of 2023 brought with it a turning point in my life—a moment when I had to confront the chaos of living with a partner battling severe mental health issues."

I'm not here to tell you a neat recovery story with Instagram-worthy milestones. I'm here to share the messy, complicated, darkly hilarious truth of surviving someone else's mental illness while trying to keep your own sanity intact.

Because sometimes the only way to process trauma is to turn it into art. And sometimes art looks like comics about sticky note security clearance systems.
